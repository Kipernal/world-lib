ColorBackInserterIteratorIterator that behaves like std::back_insert_iterator. Basically, the = operator takes a full 32-bit color and rearranges it as necessary. Depending on the iterator type, it will either split up the 32-bit colors into their individual components (in case you need to interface with something that expects a pointer to chars and don't want to have to worry about endianness), or just insert it normally
